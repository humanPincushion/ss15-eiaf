#mixism

The social playlist builder.

What the fudge?

Ever wanted to collaborate on a music playlist with your pals? Random strangers?
Already share tracks you like to your social networks?

#mixism collects tracks you shared to your social networks and builds playlists for you to listen to.

How do I use it?

First, find a cool song online...

Share the song on your social network of choice* with the #mixism hashtag (and any others you want to add)...

Sweet track! Hot Chip's cover of William Onyeabor is so good #mixism #cooltunes https://t.co/VVzdo0yHef

Share the song on your social network of choice* with the #mixism hashtag (and any others you want to add)...

You can then view and listen to the playlist on #mixism.

Pretty easy, huh?

Anyone else can contribute to the playlist by using the same hashtag...

Where/what can I share?

At the moment as proof of concept #mixism will only search Twitter for shared music hosted on SoundCloud.

We're looking to support more social networks and media platforms in the near future.

Why would I do that?

Why not :)
